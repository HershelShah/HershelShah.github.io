About Me

I'm a Senior GPU Software Engineer at Roche Diagnostics with a passion for high-performance computing and real-time systems. With over 4 years of specialized experience in GPU programming, I design and implement cutting-edge solutions for DNA sequencing and computational biology.

My expertise lies in developing high-throughput data pipelines using C++, CUDA, and modern parallel programming techniques. I've architected systems capable of processing up to 12 GB/s of real-time data, optimized CUDA kernels achieving 25% performance improvements, and built robust CI/CD workflows for GPU applications.

I hold a B.S. in Electrical Engineering from UC Davis and have worked on diverse projects ranging from computational holography to RF testing systems. I'm currently seeking new opportunities where I can apply my GPU programming expertise to solve challenging technical problems.
