## About Me

I'm a software engineer currently working at Annapurna Labs (AWS) on the Elastic Collectives Team. I'm contributing to the [aws-ofi-nccl](https://github.com/aws/aws-ofi-nccl) plugin which helps accelerate ML inference and training on AWS hardware under the github account of [hershys-aws](https://github.com/hershys-aws).

Before AWS I spent three years at Roche Diagnostics building real-time GPU pipelines for DNA sequencing — 8&nbsp;GB/s GPU-to-disk I/O with GPUDirect Storage, 12&nbsp;GB/s packet ingest over Mellanox NICs, and a stack of fused CUDA kernels that cut PCIe traffic 4&times;. Before that, holography-based 3D printing at Prellis Biologics (patent + award above), and a few summers of wireless/RF systems work at Tesla and Dolby.

Aside from work I enjoy music, particularly EDM and Jazz, running, weightlifting and travelling.
